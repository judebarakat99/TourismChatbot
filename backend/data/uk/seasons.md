# UK Seasons & Best Time to Visit

## Spring (March–May)
- Mild temperatures, flowers blooming.
- Good for city walks, parks, and countryside visits.
- Events like Chelsea Flower Show.

## Summer (June–August)
- Warmest months, longer daylight.
- Best for festivals, beaches, outdoor events.
- Very busy tourist season, especially London and Edinburgh.

## Autumn (September–November)
- Cooler weather, colorful leaves.
- Fewer tourists, good for sightseeing.
- Food and harvest festivals.

## Winter (December–February)
- Cold, short days; occasional snow.
- Christmas markets and New Year celebrations.
- Good time for museums and indoor attractions.
