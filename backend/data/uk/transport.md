# UK Transportation Guide

## Cities
- Buses, underground trains (Tube), and trams operate in major cities.
- London uses contactless cards and Oyster cards for transport.
- Taxis and ride-hailing apps like Uber are common.

## Between Cities
- Trains connect major cities (London, Manchester, Birmingham, Edinburgh).
- Coaches (National Express, Megabus) are cheaper but slower.
- Domestic flights exist but trains are often more convenient.

## Driving
- Cars drive on the left side of the road.
- Valid license required; international drivers may need an IDP.
- Parking in cities can be expensive and limited.
