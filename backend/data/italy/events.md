# UK Festivals & Seasonal Events

## January – March
- **Burns Night (25 Jan, Scotland)**  
  Celebrates poet Robert Burns with traditional food, poetry, and music.
- **Six Nations Rugby (Feb–Mar)**  
  Major rugby tournament with matches across England, Scotland, and Wales.

## April – June
- **London Marathon (April)**  
  International marathon with crowds lining central London streets.
- **Chelsea Flower Show (May, London)**  
  Famous gardening and landscape exhibition.
- **Glastonbury Festival (June, Somerset)**  
  One of the world’s biggest music festivals.

## July – September
- **Edinburgh Festival Fringe (August, Scotland)**  
  World’s largest arts festival: comedy, theatre, music.
- **Notting Hill Carnival (August, London)**  
  Caribbean culture celebration with parades and music.
- **Wimbledon Tennis Championships (June–July, London)**  
  Historic tennis tournament.

## October – December
- **Halloween Events (October)**  
  Haunted tours, ghost walks in York and Edinburgh.
- **Bonfire Night (5 Nov)**  
  Fireworks and bonfires across the UK.
- **Christmas Markets (Dec)**  
  Popular in London, Manchester, Birmingham, Edinburgh.
